Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before enrolling. Can't find an answer? Reach out to us!

Is this course suitable for complete beginners? +

Absolutely! CodeNest is designed from the ground up for beginners. We assume zero prior knowledge and walk you through every concept step-by-step. Whether you've never written a line of code or have a little experience, this course will meet you where you are and take you to the next level.

Will I get job support after completing the course? +

Yes! We provide comprehensive job support including resume and portfolio reviews, mock technical interviews, LinkedIn profile optimization, and direct referrals to our network of 200+ hiring partner companies. Our career team is dedicated to helping you land your first developer role.

Is there lifetime access to the course content? +

Yes! Once enrolled, you get lifetime access to all current course materials plus every future update we add. The tech world evolves fast, and we keep our content up-to-date so your skills always stay relevant. No recurring fees, ever.

How long will it take to complete the course? +

The course is self-paced, so it depends on how much time you dedicate. Most students who spend 2–3 hours per day complete the full curriculum in 4–6 months. You can go faster or slower — there are no deadlines, and you can rewatch any lesson as many times as you need.

Do I get a certificate upon completion? +

Yes! You'll receive an industry-recognized CodeNest Certificate of Completion once you finish all modules and submit your final capstone project. This certificate can be shared on LinkedIn and added to your resume to validate your skills to employers.

What if I'm not satisfied with the course? +

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If you try the course and feel it's not the right fit within the first 30 days, simply contact our support team and we'll process a full refund. Your satisfaction is our top priority.
